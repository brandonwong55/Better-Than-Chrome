## About

I want to thank [edelvarden](https://github.com/edelvarden/), the creator of [MaterialFox UPDATED](https://github.com/edelvarden/material-fox-updated/), for providing a basis for this theme.

I would also like to thank [MrOtherGuy](https://github.com/MrOtherGuy/) for [firefox-csshacks](https://github.com/MrOtherGuy/firefox-csshacks), along with [u/mrandish](https://www.reddit.com/user/mrandish/) for their [findbar mod](https://www.reddit.com/r/FirefoxCSS/comments/1qb7lio/sharing_back_my_css_for_findbar_with_icon_buttons/), and [FadeMind](https://github.com/FadeMind/) for their [smooth scrolling mod](https://github.com/FadeMind/Pimp-My-Fox).

Most of this theme was vibe-coded, since I have little to no knowledge of CSS. Please refer to the installation and theming instructions for MaterialFox UPDATED.

## Comments

I have no guarantees whether this will work, but my theme is working as of Firefox version 148.0
